MC Business Guide
Which MC businesses are worth your time

MC Businesses are the original passive income system in GTA Online. While they've been overshadowed by the Nightclub in terms of efficiency, certain MC businesses are still worth owning — both for direct income and for Nightclub warehouse technician assignments.

Only Cocaine and Meth are worth actively supplying and selling. The other three are only useful for Nightclub technician assignments.

Upgrade Priority

Each MC business needs upgrades to be viable:

  • Equipment Upgrade — Essential. Doubles production speed.
  • Staff Upgrade — Important. Reduces supply consumption rate.
  • Security Upgrade — Optional. Slightly reduces raid frequency.

Without upgrades, MC businesses are barely profitable.

The Raid Problem

MC businesses get raided frequently. Never register as an MC President unless you're actively doing business work. Once registered as MC, raids can happen at any time if you have product.

💡 Pro Tip: Only register as an MC President when you're ready to sell. The rest of the time, stay registered as a CEO or in passive mode.

Why Still Bother?

Despite their issues, MC businesses are worth owning for two reasons:

  1. Nightclub Technicians — You need to own Coke, Meth, and Cash businesses to assign Nightclub technicians.
  2. 2x Money Weeks — When MC businesses are on double money, full cocaine sales net $840,000.

Final Thoughts

Buy Coke and Meth for Nightclub assignments. Upgrade them if you plan to actively sell during 2x weeks. Skip Weed and Document Forgery.
