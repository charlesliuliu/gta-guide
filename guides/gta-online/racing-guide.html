Racing Guide & RP Tips
Dominate the streets of Los Santos

Racing is one of the most rewarding activities in GTA Online. Good racers earn massive RP, unlock vehicle trade prices, and build reputation in the racing community. This guide covers everything from basic racing lines to advanced techniques.

Vehicle Tuning for Races

Proper tuning makes a significant difference in race performance:

  • Transmission — Always use race transmission for fastest acceleration
  • Engine — Engine Level 4 is the goal, but level 3 is acceptable for budget builds
  • Turbo — Always install turbo charging
  • Suspension — Lower suspension improves handling at the cost of off-road capability
  • Brakes — Race brakes give meaningful stopping power improvement
💡 Pro Tip: Tires matter more than most players realize. Off-road tires actually improve traction on curbs and uneven surfaces in street races.

Racing Lines & Techniques

Master these techniques to shave seconds off your lap times:

  • Late Apex Braking — Brake later than you think you need to. Trail brake into corners for faster rotation.
  • Slow In, Fast Out — Prioritize exit speed over entry speed. A good exit is worth 0.3-0.5 seconds per corner.
  • Corner Curb Usage — Use curbs to maintain momentum, but avoid hopping over them — this loses traction.
  • Drafting — Stay within 0.5 seconds of the car ahead to get a speed boost. Use it to pass on straights.
  • Catch Slipstream — Time your exit from a draft for maximum speed boost onto the next straight.

Non-Contact Racing

Many competitive racing crews use non-contact rules. In this mode:

  • Cars pass through each other — No collision damage
  • Pure racing skill wins — No griefing or pit maneuvers
  • Better for learning — Focus on lines and braking, not defense

RP Farming Through Racing

Racing is one of the fastest ways to earn RP. Here's how to maximize RP gain:

  • Complete at least one race per day for the daily bonus
  • Stunt Races give the best RP/time ratio
  • Longer races with more players give bonus RP for number of competitors
  • Winning is less important than finishing — a clean 4th place earns more RP than a DNF

Best Race Vehicles by Class

  • Super — Grotti Itali RSX or Progen Emerus
  • Sports — Grotti Itali GTO Stinger TT
  • Muscle — Dominator ASP
  • Motorcycles — Hakuchou Drag
  • Off-Road — Vapid Trophy Truck

Final Thoughts

Racing rewards practice. Spend time in custom races learning tracks before competing. Join a racing crew to learn from experienced players and participate in organized events.
