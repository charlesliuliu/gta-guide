Facility & Avenger Guide
Doomsday operations and utility

The Facility is a specialized property primarily used for the Doomsday Heist. It also serves as a garage for special vehicles and provides access to the Avenger.

Facility Locations

Facilities cost $1,250,000 to $2,350,000. Route 68 ($1,450,000) offers the best balance of price and central location. Grand Senora Desert ($1,250,000) is cheapest but far from the city.

The Avenger

The Avenger is a customizable aircraft that serves as a mobile command center. It costs $3,450,000 or $2,300,000 after completing the Doomsday Heist. Features include:

  • Vehicle Workshop — Customize weaponized vehicles anywhere
  • Heavy Weapons — Equip explosive cannons for air superiority
  • Autopilot Hover Mode — Hover in place for precision shooting

Is It Worth It?

For most players, the Facility is only worth buying if you plan to run the Doomsday Heist. Unlike the Nightclub or Bunker, it doesn't generate passive income. The Avenger's vehicle workshop is its most practical feature for daily use.

Final Thoughts

The Facility is a niche purchase. Essential for Doomsday Heist enthusiasts but offers no passive income. Buy it for the content, not for profit.
