Mission Unlock Guide
Every contact and their missions

Contact missions are the backbone of GTA Online's PvE content. Each contact offers a unique set of missions that unlock at specific rank thresholds. This guide lists every contact, their missions, and what you need to play them.

Early Contacts (Rank 1-20)

  • Gerald — Available from rank 1. Missions include Pier Pressure, Deal Breaker, Out of Court Settlement. Good for beginners.
  • Simeon — Rank 1+. Vehicle recovery missions. Blow Up and Simeon Says are classics.
  • Lamar — Rank 5+. Lowrider missions. Community outreach and long-term contracts.

Mid-Level Contacts (Rank 20-50)

  • Martin Madrazo — Rank 15+. Dispatch missions, Extradition, and Diamonds are for Trevor.
  • Ron Jakowski — Rank 24+. Trevor Philips Industries missions. Base invaders and Hangar management.
  • Trevor Philips — Rank 20+. Scorched Earth, Crystal Clear Out, and Death From Above.

High-Level Contacts (Rank 50+)

  • Lester Crest — Rank 35+. Assassination missions. Trash Talk and By the Book are popular.
  • Tom Connors (The Truth) — Rank 40+. Smuggler missions. High-paying but difficult.
  • Agent 14 — Rank 60+. Doomsday setup contact. Gunrunning missions.

Unlock Requirements

Some missions have specific unlock conditions:

  • Original Heists — Require high-end apartment and rank 12
  • Doomsday Heist — Requires Facility property, any rank
  • Casino Heist — Requires Arcade property, any rank
  • Cayo Perico Heist — Requires Kosatka sub, rank 0+ (no rank requirement)

Final Thoughts

Contact missions are best played with 2-4 players on hard difficulty. They pay well during double money weeks and are excellent for building rank and capital in your first few weeks.
