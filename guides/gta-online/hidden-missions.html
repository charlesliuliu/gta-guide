Hidden Missions & Easter Eggs
Every secret in Los Santos

GTA Online is filled with hidden content that most players never discover. From random events that pay thousands to easter eggs referencing Rockstar's game history, this guide catalogs every secret worth knowing about.

Random Events

Random events spawn in freemode and offer quick cash, RP, and rare items:

  • Blue Dot Events — Random encounters on the map. Pay $10,000-$30,000.
  • Smuggler Planes — Destroy cargo planes for $10,000-$20,000 and weapon parts.
  • Business Battles — Public lobby events involving contraband pickups. Good for supplies.
  • Crime Scene — Find police dead drops and return the evidence for cash.

Hidden Weapon Acquisition

  • Navy Revolver — Solve a series of clues across the map. Free weapon.
  • Stone Hatchet — Complete the Maude bounties. Free weapon with unique melee ability.
  • Double-Action Revolver — Complete the Treasure Hunt. Free weapon, pays $250,000.
  • Baseball Bat — Find behind a specific building in Vespucci Beach.

Easter Eggs

  • Alien Egg — Found in specific locations during certain in-game time windows.
  • Bigfoot Peek — Rare sighting in the Paleto Forest at night.
  • Ghost Girl — Appears on Mount Gordo at 11 PM in-game time.
  • Infinity Killer — Hidden murder mystery across the map.
  • Yeti — Huntable creature in the Chiliad wilderness.

Final Thoughts

Hidden content adds depth to GTA Online beyond the main activities. The weapon-related unlocks (Navy Revolver, Stone Hatchet) are essential for every player's arsenal and take less than an hour to complete.
