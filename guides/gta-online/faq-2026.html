GTA Online FAQ 2026
Answers to the most common questions

After years of updates and changes, GTA Online can be overwhelming for new and returning players. This FAQ covers the most common questions we hear from the community.

Getting Started

Q: How do I make my first million dollars fast?

A: Complete the tutorial, do contact missions until you can buy a high-end apartment, run the Fleeca Job, then save for the Kosatka submarine. Cayo Perico Heist is the fastest path to your first million.

Q: What should I buy first?

A: High-end apartment ($205,000) → Armored Kuruma ($525,000) → CEO Office ($1,000,000) → Kosatka ($2,200,000). In that exact order.

Business Questions

Q: Which business is best for passive income?

A: The Nightclub. With equipment and staff upgrades, it generates $80,000-$160,000 per hour while you do other activities. No supplies needed.

Q: Should I steal or buy supplies?

A: Buy supplies. The time spent stealing supplies is better spent on activities that earn more money.

Money & Progression

Q: What's the fastest way to level up?

A: Double RP events are fastest. Stunt races and contact missions are consistent alternatives.

Q: Is Shark Cards worth it?

A: No. A $20 Shark Card gives you 200,000 GTA dollars. The same amount can be earned in 30 minutes with Cayo Perico.

Final Thoughts

GTA Online in 2026 is the most content-rich it's ever been. Take your time, follow a structured progression path, and don't let griefers discourage you. The game rewards persistence.
