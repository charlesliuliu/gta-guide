Best Method By Playtime

Choose based on how long you can play:

  • 15-30 minutes — Payphone Hits + Vehicle Cargo. Quick, consistent, good payout.
  • 30-60 minutes — Cayo Perico Heist. Best payout per session.
  • 60-120 minutes — Cayo Perico + Casino Heist rotation. Maximum efficiency.
  • AFK / Idle — Nightclub warehouse. Pure passive income.

Final Thoughts

The most efficient players layer multiple activities. Run a Cayo Perico finale, immediately source a vehicle cargo, then complete a Payphone Hit during cooldowns. This layered approach maximizes every minute online.
