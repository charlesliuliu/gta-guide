Top 5 Most Profitable Activities in GTA Online (2026)

Los Santos Guide • Updated 2026

💰
Most Profitable GTA Online Activities
Maximize your dollars per hour in 2026

Not all money in GTA Online is created equal. Some activities pay millions per hour while others barely cover ammunition costs. We've crunched the numbers on every major moneymaking activity in the game and ranked them by raw payout per hour. Here are the top 5 most profitable things you can do in GTA Online right now.

#1: Cayo Perico Heist — $1,200,000 to $1,600,000 per hour

The Cayo Perico Heist remains the undisputed king of GTA Online moneymaking in 2026. With the Kosatka submarine, you can run this heist entirely solo in about 45-60 minutes from start to finish, earning between $1.2 million and $1.6 million depending on the primary target and secondary loot.

The key to maximizing Cayo Perico efficiency is learning the optimal route. Enter through the drainage tunnel, use the conspirator weapon loadout, and memorize guard patterns. With practice, you can complete the entire heist in under 40 minutes including setups. That's nearly $2 million per hour at maximum efficiency.

💡 Pro Tip: Always scout for cocaine and gold in the compound. If you don't get good secondary loot, cancel the heist and pay the $25,000 setup fee again. It's worth it for the extra $200,000+ payout.

#2: The Diamond Casino Heist — $800,000 to $1,200,000 per hour

The Casino Heist is the second best moneymaker when run with a partner. The optimal approach is Silent & Sneaky with the Gruppe Sechs disguise and the sewer exit. With two experienced players, you can clear the vault of its maximum $3.6 million take in about 10 minutes.

If you're running VIP artwork, you only need two players and the hacking is minimal. The setups take about 45 minutes and the finale takes 10-12 minutes. Even with setups, you're looking at $800,000-$1,000,000 per hour split between two players.

#3: Vehicle Cargo — $400,000 per hour (solo)

CEO Vehicle Cargo remains one of the best solo grinding activities. The strategy is simple: source high-end vehicles only by maintaining a warehouse of standard and mid-range cars without selling them. Once your warehouse has 10 standard and 10 mid-range cars, the game will only source top-range vehicles worth $80,000 each (increased to $100,000 for distant sales).

With a well-placed vehicle warehouse near La Mesa or Murrieta Heights, you can source and sell a top-range car every 15 minutes. That's $320,000 per hour with the high-demand bonus, and it requires no other players.

#4: Nightclub Passive Income — $80,000-$160,000 per hour (AFK)

The Nightclub is unique because it generates money while you're doing other things. With a fully upgraded nightclub warehouse and five technicians assigned to the top businesses (Cargo, Cocaine, Meth, Counterfeit Cash, Bunker), your nightclub will accrue goods worth approximately $80,000-$160,000 per hour while you sleep, work, or play other games.

The key is to AFK properly. Watch a security camera or start a contact mission and sit in your apartment. Configure your console/PC to not turn off and check in every 12-16 hours to sell. A full nightclub warehouse sells for $1.7 million in a single sale mission.

⚠ Note: AFK methods may violate Rockstar's EULA if done through certain glitches. The security camera or contact mission method is low-risk and has been widely used for years without bans.

#5: Weekly Double/Triple Money Events

Every Thursday, Rockstar updates the weekly bonuses. Featured gamemodes, contact missions, and heists rotate on double or triple money. When a specific activity is boosted, it often becomes the best moneymaker in the game for that week.

For example, when Contact Missions are 3x money, completing them on hard difficulty with a group of 4 can earn $400,000-$600,000 per hour. When Double Money Heists are active, repeating the finales of the original heists can push $600,000-$900,000 per hour.

Honorable Mentions

  • Payphone Hits — $85,000 for 5 minutes of work with the Professional hit bonus
  • Agency Security Contracts — $70,000-$80,000 for 10-minute missions. Dr. Dre contract pays $1,000,000 on first completion.
  • Acid Lab — New passive income source with the Acid Lab business from the Drug Wars update. $350,000-$500,000 per full bar.
  • Bunker Manufacturing — $175,000 profit per full bar when selling to Los Santos. Especially good during 2x bunker weeks.
  • Auto Shop Customer Cars and Deliveries — $60,000-$160,000 for minimal effort between activities.

Optimizing Your Routine

The best approach is to layer multiple income streams. Start a nightclub sale, then run the nightclub popularity mission. While those are ticking, source and sell vehicle cargo. In between vehicle cargo cooldowns, run a Cayo Perico setup. When you need a break from active grinding, let the nightclub and acid lab generate passive income.

Professional grinders combine Cayo Perico with the Casino Heist in a rotation. They run Cayo Perico solo for 45 minutes, then immediately start Casino Heist setups with a partner. This layered approach pushes earnings to $2+ million per hour.

Final Thoughts

The meta changes slightly with every weekly update, but Cayo Perico has remained the top moneymaker since its release. If you only have time for one activity, make it Cayo Perico. If you can play for 2-3 hours, combine Cayo Perico with vehicle cargo and nightclub sales for maximum efficiency.
