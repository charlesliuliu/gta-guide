Diamond Casino Heist Guide
All approaches compared

The Diamond Casino Heist is GTA Online's most flexible heist. With three distinct approaches and dozens of possible entry and exit combinations, every playthrough can feel different. More importantly, it pays exceptionally well — a well-executed Casino Heist with artwork on hard mode pays over $2,000,000 per player. This guide covers everything you need to run it flawlessly.

Prerequisites

To unlock the Casino Heist, you need to own a high-end apartment with a heist room, have completed the Fleeca Job, and visit the Casino for the first time. After that, Lester will invite you to his garment factory, and the heist becomes available.

You also need to buy an Arcade property to plan the heist. The cheapest is the Pixel Pete's in Grapeseed for $1,235,000, but the Videogeddon in La Mesa ($1,875,000) is the best location — it's closest to the Casino, making setup missions much faster.

Approach 1: Silent & Sneaky (Recommended)

This is the fastest and highest-paying approach when done correctly. The key is the Gruppe Sechs disguise — you enter through the staff lobby dressed as security, bypassing the front door entirely.

Optimal loadout: Silenced weapons only. Use the EMP device to disable cameras in the hallway. The trickiest part is navigating the security room without alerting the guards. Time your movements with guard patrol patterns.

💡 Pro Tip: For the vault door, both the cutting torch and the electric drill work. The cutting torch is faster but requires a setup mission. Always prioritize the cutting torch if you have the patience for the extra setup.

Approach 2: The Big Con

The Big Con is the most forgiving approach. You enter disguised as maintenance staff, casino security, or Gruppe Sechs officers. The optimal disguise depends on the vault contents:

  • Gruppe Sechs — Best for artwork, quick entry through staff lobby
  • Maintenance — Good all-rounder, enter through the maintenance tunnels
  • Noise Bomb Decoy — Third option, creates a distraction

The Big Con allows you to walk through the casino floor without raising alarm. Simply walk to the staff entrance, take the elevator down, and access the vault. The disguise holds as long as you don't run, attack anyone, or get too close to certain NPCs.

Approach 3: Aggressive

The Aggressive approach is straightforward but lowest paying. You enter through the front doors with heavy weapons, take hostages, and fight your way to the vault. The payout is lower because cash is the most common vault contents with this approach.

This approach is best for beginners learning the heist mechanics. There's no stealth to worry about — just shoot everything that moves. Use the assault shotgun and combat MG for maximum damage.

Payout Optimization

To maximize your payout, follow these priorities:

  1. Scope the vault contents first. Artwork is best, gold is second, cash is worst.
  2. If vault contents are cash, consider canceling and trying again.
  3. Choose the approach based on vault contents, not personal preference.
  4. Always play on hard mode. The extra 10% payout is worth the one-life restriction.
  5. During the escape, take the sewer exit to lose the cops instantly.

Best Exit Routes

  • Sewer Exit — Best overall. Drive down the elevator shaft, through the sewers, and emerge by the casino. Lose cops immediately.
  • Staff Lobby — Good for Big Con. Exit through the same door you entered.
  • Race Track — Requires a getaway vehicle. Follow the race track route to the underground parking.
  • Roof / Helicopter — Use the NOOSE helicopter if available. Fastest exit but requires a specific prep.

Hacking Tips

The Casino Heist requires fingerprint hacking. Practice these patterns:

  • Learn the common print patterns. Four fingers, find the match.
  • The hack timer pauses when you're scrolling the pattern selector.
  • Use lower difficulties for practice. Hard mode has more fingerprints.
  • With practice, each hack takes 5-8 seconds. There are typically 4-5 hacks total.
💡 Pro Tip: If hacking is slowing you down, get the time/price reduction vault drills from the Arcade's Master Control Terminal. Level 2 hacking is significantly easier than level 1.

Elite Challenge

The elite challenge requirements for the Casino Heist are:

  • Complete in under 15 minutes (from entering casino to leaving)
  • No restarts
  • No player deaths in the finale
  • Empty the vault completely

The elite bonus is $100,000. It's achievable with practice. The key is emptying the vault quickly and taking the optimal exit route.

Final Thoughts

The Casino Heist is a fantastic complement to Cayo Perico. While Cayo Perico is better for solo players, the Casino Heist with a skilled partner can rival its payout. The variety in approaches keeps it from feeling repetitive, and the $3.6 million maximum take makes it worth the setup time. Master all three approaches and you'll always have a profitable heist ready to run.
