Weapons & Armor Guide
Best loadouts for every situation

Choosing the right weapons in GTA Online can mean the difference between a smooth heist and a respawn screen. With dozens of weapons available at various ranks, it's important to know which ones are worth buying and which are a waste of money.

Essential Weapons by Category

Assault Rifles

WeaponRankPriceBest For

Sniper Rifles

  • Pistol .50 — Best sidearm, high damage
  • AP Pistol — Drive-by weapon, use in vehicles
  • Combat PDW — Good for tight spaces
  • Up-n-Atomizer — Utility tool, pushes vehicles and enemies

Heavy Weapons

  • Homing Launcher — Anti-air, lock-on missiles
  • Compact Grenade Launcher — Vehicle destruction, area denial
  • Minigun — Rank 120, highest DPS in the game

Armor & Utility

Always carry maximum armor and snacks:

  • Super Heavy Body Armor ($500) — Stack up to 10
  • Snacks from convenience stores or office assistant
  • Molotovs — Good for crowd control
  • Sticky Bombs — Vehicle destruction, heist emergencies

Weapon Locker Setup

Use the weapon locker in your property to hide weapons you don't use:

  • Keep visible: Special Carbine, Pump Shotgun Mk II, Heavy Sniper Mk II, AP Pistol, Combat MG Mk II, Sticky Bombs, Grenades
  • Hide: All starting weapons, duplicate weapons, weapons below your level
💡 Pro Tip: Using a weapon locker to hide unwanted weapons significantly improves weapon switching speed during combat.

Final Thoughts

Buy the Special Carbine first, then save for the Carbine Rifle Mk II at rank 42. The Heavy Sniper Mk II with thermal scope is essential for PvP at rank 90+.
