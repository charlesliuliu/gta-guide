Best GTA V Mods 2026

Best GTA V Mods 2026
Top mods for every category

GTA V on PC has one of the most vibrant modding communities in gaming. From photorealistic graphics overhauls to absurd gameplay additions, here are the best mods available in 2026.

Essential Graphics Mods

  • NaturalVision Evolved (NVE) — The most comprehensive graphics overhaul. Updated lighting, reflections, and weather.
  • QuantV — Cinematic visual pack with advanced post-processing.
  • VisualV — Subtler graphics enhancement. More performance-friendly than NVE.
  • ReShade — Post-processing injector. Customize color grading, sharpening, and effects.

Gameplay Overhauls

  • LSPDFR (Los Santos Police Department First Response) — Become a police officer. Full career system.
  • Vice Cry — Replaces nearly all game files with improved versions. Massive visual upgrade.
  • Menyoo PC — Single-player spawn menu. Spawn vehicles, weapons, NPCs, and more.
  • Enhanced Native Trainer — Better than Menyoo for basic spawning and player manipulation.

Vehicle & Weapon Mods

  • Realistic Driving V — Overhauls vehicle handling. More weight, better physics.
  • Add-on Vehicle Packs — Thousands of real-world car models available.
  • Weapon Models Overhaul — HD weapon skins and real-world weapon replacements.

Performance & Utility

  • Heap Adjuster — Fixes memory allocation issues. Essential for heavy mod setups.
  • Packfile Limit Adjuster — Removes the game's file size limitation.
  • OpenIV — Essential modding tool. Required for installing most mods.
  • Script Hook V — Required for script-based mods like LSPDFR and trainers.

Installation Guide

  1. Install OpenIV — The foundation for GTA V modding
  2. Install Script Hook V — Required for gameplay mods
  3. Install a trainer (Menyoo or Enhanced Native Trainer) — For testing mods
  4. Add your chosen mods — Follow each mod's specific installation instructions
  5. Use OpenIV's mod manager — Keep your installation organized

Final Thoughts

Modding GTA V can transform the game into a completely new experience. Start with NaturalVision Evolved for visuals, then explore LSPDFR for a full career experience. Always back up your game files before modding.
