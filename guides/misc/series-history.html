Notable Spin-offs

  • GTA: Liberty City Stories (2005) — PSP launch title, solid portable GTA
  • GTA: Vice City Stories (2006) — Underrated prequel with empire building
  • GTA: Chinatown Wars (2009) — Innovative top-down entry for Nintendo DS
  • GTA: The Trilogy — Definitive Edition (2021) — Controversial remaster, later patched

GTA V Sales Milestones

  • 2013 — $1 billion in 3 days (fastest entertainment launch in history)
  • 2017 — 70 million copies sold
  • 2024 — 190 million copies sold
  • GTA V is the second best-selling video game of all time behind Minecraft

Final Thoughts

The GTA series has consistently pushed the boundaries of what video games can achieve. Each entry builds on the last, and GTA VI represents the culmination of 25 years of open-world expertise.
