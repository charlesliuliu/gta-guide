GTA6 Dual Protagonist System Explained

Los Santos Guide • Updated 2026

👫
GTA6 Dual Protagonist System
Bonnie & Clyde in Vice City

GTA6 introduces a new take on the multiple protagonist system first seen in GTA V. This time, the story follows Lucia and Jason, a Bonnie & Clyde inspired couple navigating the criminal underworld of Vice City.

Character Details

  • Lucia — First female protagonist in mainline GTA history. Appears in orange prison jumpsuit in the trailer.
  • Jason — Male counterpart, Lucia's partner in crime. Less is known about his background.
  • Relationship Dynamic — Romantic partnership, cooperation-based gameplay, shared objectives

Gameplay Implications

  • Squad-based heists — Coordinate with your partner for two-person heist approaches
  • Relationship system — Partner actions and story choices affect relationship and outcomes
  • Switching mechanics — Similar to GTA V, instant switch between Lucia and Jason
  • Shared progression — Skills and resources are shared between both characters

Final Thoughts

The dual protagonist system adds emotional depth to the narrative. Rockstar is known for character-driven stories, and Lucia and Jason represent the most ambitious character work since Arthur Morgan in Red Dead Redemption 2.
