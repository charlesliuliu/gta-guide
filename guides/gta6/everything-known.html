GTA6 — Everything We Know So Far (2026)

GTA6 Everything Known
Complete information roundup

Grand Theft Auto VI is the most anticipated video game in history. Here's everything we know about it based on official trailers, reliable leaks, and industry analysis. This page is updated as new information emerges.

Official Details

  • Setting — Return to Vice City (fictional Miami)
  • Protagonists — Two characters, Lucia and Jason, in a Bonnie & Clyde inspired story
  • Release Window — Fall 2025 (currently projected, delays possible)
  • Platforms — PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S at launch
  • Trailer 1 — Released December 2023, 90 seconds, 90+ million views

Map Size & Scope

Based on leaked development footage, the GTA6 map is significantly larger than GTA V's Los Santos. Estimates suggest a map that is 2-3 times the size of GTA V, covering Vice City, surrounding wetlands, and multiple smaller towns.

Gameplay Changes

  • Dual protagonist switching — Similar to GTA V, but with a romantic partnership dynamic
  • Expanded interior access — More enterable buildings than any previous GTA
  • Dynamic world events — Random encounters and evolving world state
  • Enhanced police AI — More realistic law enforcement behaviors

Final Thoughts

GTA6 represents a generational leap for the franchise. With Rockstar's track record, expectations are incredibly high. Stay tuned for more details as they emerge.
