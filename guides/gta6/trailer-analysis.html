GTA6 Trailer 1 Analysis
Every frame decoded

Rockstar released the first GTA6 trailer in December 2023, and it was immediately dissected by millions of fans. The 90-second trailer is packed with details that hint at the game's setting, story, and scope. Here's our complete frame-by-frame breakdown.

Setting Confirmation

The trailer confirms Vice City as the primary setting. Key location details:

  • Vice City skyline — Modernized version of the classic GTA: Vice City skyline
  • Ocean Drive — Colorful Art Deco buildings along a beachfront strip
  • Wetlands / Everglades — Airboat shots showing swamp environments
  • Beaches — Crowded beaches with detailed NPC activities
  • Suburban areas — Residential neighborhoods with pools and yachts

Characters Revealed

Two protagonists are shown:

  • Lucia — Seen in prison clothing, then later free. Implies the story begins with her release.
  • Jason — Shown alongside Lucia in multiple scenes. Their relationship is central to the story.
  • The trailer's song 'Love Is a Long Road' by Tom Petty reinforces the Bonnie & Clyde theme.

Gameplay Teases

  • Social media UI — Phone interface shows a social media app called 'Bleeter' (parody of X/Twitter)
  • Live-streaming culture — NPCs filming with phones, influencer culture references
  • Large crowds — Beach scenes with dozens of NPCs suggests improved NPC density
  • Wildlife — Flamingos, alligators, and other animals visible
  • Dynamic weather — Rain, sun, and stormy conditions shown

Final Thoughts

The trailer confirms GTA6 is a generational leap in fidelity and scale. The attention to detail in NPC behaviors, environmental density, and lighting suggests a level of polish only Rockstar can deliver.
