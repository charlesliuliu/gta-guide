GTA6 Map Analysis
Vice City and surrounding regions

The GTA6 map is shaping up to be the largest and most detailed Rockstar has ever created. This analysis breaks down what we know about the map's size, regions, and key landmarks.

Map Size Comparison

GameEstimated SizeSetting
GTA III3 sq milesLiberty City
GTA: Vice City4 sq milesVice City
GTA: San Andreas14 sq milesSan Andreas (state)

GTA VI (est.)60-90 sq milesVice City + wetlands + islands

Regions

  • Vice City (Urban Core) — Downtown, beaches, Art Deco district, Vice Beach
  • Wetlands / Everglades — Swamp regions with airboat access, rural communities
  • Islands / Keys — Tropical islands, resort areas, hidden coves
  • Surrounding Suburbs — Residential areas, shopping districts, industrial zones

Final Thoughts

The GTA6 map is designed to feel alive and reactive. Dynamic weather, wildlife, and NPC behaviors will make the world feel more immersive than any previous GTA title.
