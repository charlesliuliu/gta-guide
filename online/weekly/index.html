Rotation Patterns

Rockstar uses a semi-predictable rotation for weekly bonuses. Based on historical data from 2024–2026:

  • Every 4–6 weeks — Cayo Perico or Casino Heist bonuses return
  • Every 8–12 weeks — Bunker or MC businesses get 2x sales
  • Quarterly — New content drops (vehicles, missions, QoL features)
  • Seasonal — Halloween, Christmas, Summer special events
📈 Historical Insight

In 2025, Rockstar introduced a pattern of alternating between "heist bonuses" and "business bonuses" every 2 weeks. If this week is business-focused, expect heist bonuses next week — and vice versa.

Maximizing Weekly Events

To get the most out of each weekly rotation:

  1. Log in Thursday morning

    Check the newswire or this page for the latest bonuses. The first 24 hours of a new week have the most players running group content.

  2. Stack bonuses with passive income

    Run 2x missions while your Nightclub, Bunker, and Acid Lab produce passive income. Collect and sell before the week ends.

  3. Claim the freebies

    Every week there's at least one free item: a podium vehicle, LS Car Meet prize ride, or clothing. These add up to millions in savings over a year.

  4. Plan around the next week

    If you know a business bonus is coming, stock up your Bunker and MC businesses the week before so you're ready to sell at 2x.
