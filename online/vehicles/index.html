Best Utility Vehicles

🚗 Armored Kuruma — $200,000

The single best value vehicle in GTA Online. Nearly full bulletproofing against NPCs, great speed, 4 doors. Buy this before anything else — it will pay for itself through easier missions.

🚀 Oppressor MK2 — $3,890,000 (requires Nightclub + Terrorbyte)

The king of PvE grinding. Fastest point-to-point travel, can spawn instantly as MC President. Controversial in PvP, but for grinding, nothing beats it. Add missiles and countermeasures.

💰 Speedo Custom (Nightclub Sale Van) — Free with Nightclub

Your best passive income delivery vehicle. Upgrade to maximum armor and mines to protect your Nightclub cargo sales.

Beginner Buying Guide

Use this priority list for your first few million:

  1. Armored Kuruma ($200K) — Best value, makes missions easy
  2. Mk II Sparrow ($1.8M) — Fast travel from Kosatka, essential for Cayo Perico
  3. Kosatka Submarine ($2.2M) — Unlocks Cayo Perico heist, best income source
  4. Oppressor MK2 ($3.8M) — Endgame grinding vehicle, prioritize after steady income
