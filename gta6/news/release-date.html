✅ Confirmed: November 19, 2026

Rockstar Games has officially confirmed November 19, 2026 as the release date for Grand Theft Auto VI. After multiple delays — from the original Fall 2025 window, to May 26, 2026, and finally to November — the date is locked.

📈 The Delay Timeline

2022 — Development begins in earnest after RDR2 support winds down
2023.12 — Trailer 1 confirms 2025 release window
2024 — Internal delays push target to 2026
2025.05 — Trailer 2 released, "2026" confirmed verbally
2025.09 — Rockstar officially announces delay: May 26, 2026
2025.11 — Second delay announced: November 19, 2026
Now — Current target is locked

Rockstar's statement regarding the November delay reads: "We are very sorry that this is later than you expected. We need this extra time to deliver at the level of quality you expect and deserve." The game will launch exclusively on PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S.

GTA6's development cycle is the longest in Rockstar's history — nearly 3 years from first trailer to launch. This reflects the game's unprecedented scale, technical ambition, and Rockstar's shift away from crunch toward more sustainable development practices.

  1. November 19, 2026 — Console Launch

    GTA6 launches on PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S. This is the primary release — all trailers and marketing focus on the console version.

  2. Late 2027 / Early 2028 — PC Port (Estimated)

    Following Rockstar tradition (GTA5: 1.5 year delay, RDR2: 1 year), the PC version is expected 12–18 months after console. Likely on Steam and Epic Games Store with enhanced graphics and higher frame rates.

🚀 Pre-order Guide

Pre-orders are expected to go live shortly after the final trailer (likely late summer 2026). Here's our advice:

  • Standard Edition — Best value if you don't care about cosmetics. Rockstar games don't get meaningful pre-order discounts, so there's no rush.
  • Deluxe Edition — Worth it for new players who want a GTA Online head start. The $500K bonus and exclusive vehicle help skip the early grind.
  • Ultimate Edition — Only if you're all-in. The DLC pass may not see content until 2027+.
  • Collector's Edition — For serious collectors. Expect limited stock — if you want it, pre-order the day it's announced.

❓ FAQ

Q: Is GTA6 coming to PC at launch?
No. Like GTA5 and RDR2 before it, the PC version will follow 12–18 months after the console release. Rockstar has not announced a PC date.

Q: Will GTA6 be on PS4 or Xbox One?
No. GTA6 is current-gen only: PS5 and Xbox Series X|S only. Rockstar has confirmed there will be no last-gen version.

Q: Will GTA6 Online be separate from GTA5's Online?
Yes. GTA6 Online will be a new standalone experience launching after the single-player release. GTA5 Online will continue to operate independently.

Q: Can I transfer my GTA Online character to GTA6?
Rockstar has not confirmed any cross-game progression. Given the technical differences, a full transfer is unlikely, though a loyalty bonus (cash, exclusive items) is possible.

Q: Will there be a day-one patch?
Almost certainly. Rockstar games typically have a substantial day-one patch. Physical editions will likely ship with version 1.0 and require downloading the update.

📌 Summary

GTA6 launches November 19, 2026 on PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S. After two delays, this date is locked. PC players will need to wait until at least late 2027. Pre-orders likely open late summer 2026.
